This website was created as a simple alternative to speedrun.com

SRC has become very annoying over the years. From bad user experience changes, to a costly membership that does absolutely nothing, to sketchy ads, Elo, the parent company, just doesn't seem to care about their website. For more information, check out this video by Storster.

I created this website to view the leaderboards for a game simply, and effeciently

No more dealing with skecthy ads. No more dealing with a bad user experience. No more dealing with a monthly membership. I just wanted a way to quickly view the leaderboards, and here it is.

WARNING

Please keep in mind that you can only view leaderboards. You CANNOT SUBMIT runs, VERIFY runs, or anything that has to do with the backend of SRC. This is a purely FRONTEND project.

LIMITATIONS

As of 2/12/23, you cannot filter runs other than by category (you cannot filter runs by date, whether a run has a video or not, you cannot see obsolete runs, etc.). You cannot view categories that have more than two different variables that are sub categories (e.g. Minecraft Any% Glitchless with set/random seed and different versions). This is only a small percentage of games however. I will try to implement fixes for these overtime. Please also keep in mind that I am still a dumb student and there will be glitches. Please be kind when reporting them.
